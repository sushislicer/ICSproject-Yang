Defense: 5

Range: 7

Mobility: 2

Difficulty: 1

"Torbjörn’s extensive arsenal includes a rivet gun and hammer, as well as a personal forge that he can use to build upgrade-able turrets and dole out protective armor packs."

  • Turrets go on the high ground.
  • Press 2 to take out your hammer. Use it to hit your turret until it upgrades. You don't need scrap to upgrade your turrets. Use the scrap to create an armor pack whenever it is enough.
  • Torbjorn is fragile, but he can jump up to twice his height. Use your jump to juke people.
  • When you see your turret goes into low health, use your ultimate.
  • Do not sit behind your turret forever. Go and help your team by shotgunning people. Jump while doing so, because Torbjorn's crouch is disappointing.
