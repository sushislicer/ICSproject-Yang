  • The chain is absolutely broken. You can still hook people through cars and walls if you see a tiny bit of their hitbox through the space between the car and the car tires or something. Abuse that.
  • If you hook a target, they get pulled to the position you are facing when the hook ends. Hook them, then turn towards a bottomless pit.
  • Walk forward and shoot after you hooked someone, so they don't get a chance to back away and live. Besure to melee them even after the shot for some extra finishing damage.
  • Pressing R before taking a breather can cancel half of your reload animation.
  • Whole Hog is pretty useless in terms of damage but great to push people back into a corner. If the enemy team is grouped up too closely, punish them by putting them into a corner.
