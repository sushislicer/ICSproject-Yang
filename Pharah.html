Damage: 8

Defense: 2

Range: 8

Mobility: 7

Difficulty: 4

"Soaring through the air in her combat armor, and armed with a launcher that lays down high-explosive rockets, Pharah is a force to be reckoned with."

  • Pharah is extremely vertically orientated, openning her vision up to a larger portion of the map, but being in the sky also makes her vulnerable to attack.
  • Take angles of attack, even in the air. A pharah in open air will be shot down instantly.
  • Straffing in the air is very important in order to complete your shots.
  • Concussive blast is extremely flexible and allows for greater movement around the map.
  • Pharah's verticallity allows her to abuse opennings and fly over chokepoints. Use this to your advantage.
