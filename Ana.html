  • Alternating between hitscan and projectile use is very important as Ana. You should be primarily healing with projectile but if you need to tag out an enemy or tag a pharah in the sky quickly switching to hitscan is very useful.
  • Communication sleep dart is very important. Sleep dart should also not be used as a last resort to avoid being killed, it should be used actively and launched into the choke.
  • You are Ana. Not Widowmaker. Don't sit in the back and snipe, because you are most likely just going to kill yourself.
  • Coordinate nano-boost. You don't want to boost the Lucio in front of your ulting Genji.
  • Boost order: Direct Offense Who have Ultimates Up, then Tanks, then Defense and Supports.
