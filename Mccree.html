"Using his Peacekeeper, McCree takes out targets with deadeye precision and dives out of danger with eagle-like speed."

  • McCree is a highly flexible hero who can deal up to huge amounts of damage while not sacrificing anyother trait.
  • Although fan the hammer cannot headshot, it can still deal some decent damage to barriers and escaping targets.
  • McCree is very loud, and almost any movement will give away his position.
  • Flashbang hits an area of effect around, so besure to use the splash effect accordingly.
  • McCree has no defensive abilities other than flashbang, so be careful that you do not waste your flashbang or be caught out of position
